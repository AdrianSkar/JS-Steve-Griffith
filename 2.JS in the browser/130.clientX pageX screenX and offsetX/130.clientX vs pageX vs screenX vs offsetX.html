clientX vs pageX vs screenX vs offsetX

Click on this text or on any element within the box.
  • offsetX: Distance from pointer to the padding edge of the node (element).
  • clientX: Distance from pointer to the viewport.
  • pageX: Distance from pointer to the edge of the document/page.
  • screenX: Distance from pointer to the edge of the screen (element).
