Localhost across WiFi networks

They can be served, among others, with live extensions and/or XAMPP.

Usually can be accessed following local IP schemas:

  1. http://127.0.0.1/path/file.html
  2. localhost/path/file.html
  3. http://192.168.1.255/path/file.html

192[...] addresses can be accessed from any device on the same WiFi.
